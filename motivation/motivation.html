What is Computational Thinking?

Courses in Science and Engineering focus on two approaches to problem solving:

  • Theoretical
  • Experimental

But there's a third approach:

  • Computational
  • With computational thinking, we reformulate a problem to take advantage of computers
  • ..i.e., we develop a computational solution to the problem
  • We implement our solution using programming

Computational thinking forms the basis for a lot of courses in Computer Science. But engineers and scientists from all fields are doing amazing things with computers
